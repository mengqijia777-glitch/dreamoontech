What are the Most Popular Marketing Tools in 2024?

June 2025
Marketing
5 min read

Judging from various data, Facebook is undoubtedly one of the most popular marketing platforms in 2024. Let's explore why this platform continues to dominate the digital marketing landscape.

Why Use Facebook for Marketing?

1. Massive User Base

  • Facebook has over 2.8 billion monthly active users, nearly three times that of WeChat.
  • 70% of American adults use Facebook, making it a key platform for reaching a global audience.

2. High Engagement Levels

  • Users spend 20% of their total internet time and 25% of mobile internet time on Facebook.
  • Facebook influences 80% of passive buyers, compared to only 20% for active, intent-driven searches like those on Amazon or Google.

3. Rich User Data for Precision Targeting

  • Facebook collects detailed user data, including real names, birthdays, education, work history, interests, and online activities.
  • Advertisers can precisely target users based on location, age, interests, device type, marital status, and more.
  • With an accuracy rate of 89% for ad targeting, Facebook outperforms many other platforms, whose accuracy averages around 38%.

4. Comprehensive Ecosystem of Products

  • Includes Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, WhatsApp, and Audience Network, providing multiple channels for integrated marketing campaigns.

5. Global Influence

  • Facebook is widely used even in regions with limited e-commerce platforms, such as Mongolia, where people rely on Facebook to discover new products.

6. Proven Results for Businesses

  • 80 million businesses globally use Facebook for marketing.
  • It's a versatile platform, effective for industries ranging from e-commerce and apps to education, healthcare, and government campaigns.

Key Takeaway: Facebook's combination of massive reach, precise targeting capabilities, and high user engagement makes it an essential platform for businesses looking to grow their customer base in 2024 and beyond.

For Australian small businesses, leveraging Facebook's marketing tools can provide unprecedented access to potential customers. Whether you're just starting or looking to scale your existing marketing efforts, Facebook offers the tools and audience to help you succeed.
